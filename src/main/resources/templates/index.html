    Projects for Public Security Department:

  • Command & Dispatching Platform

    • The project handles the security situation in Gansu Province through the number of cases in real time, the type of case, the number of personnel in key places, the classification and quantity of personnel in the supervision office, the number of police officers attending, the personnel entering and leaving Gansu Province, and the monitoring of cameras.     The system is a convenience service platform open to the public of the Gansu Province Public Security Department.   • Mobile Information Verification System

    • The system is an APP for police handheld terminals. The main function is that police officers can use the police handheld terminal to check the identity information of pedestrians or vehicles. By scanning the ID card or the car number, the identity information of the person or vehicle can be obtained immediately. The person or vehicle is suspected, the terminal will promptly prompt and show the disposal measures.


    Projects for Lanzhou University:

  • Campus Geographic Information System

    • The campus geographic information system (GIS) of the University of Lanzhou is based on the design concept of “visualization, refinement and intelligence” of resource management. The system is implemented by the technology of WebGIS, and is a campus infrastructure resource management system that combines the Internet of Things with the campus network. The system manages the spatial data, attribute data and business data of various infrastructure resources on campus, providing intuitive data for campus resource planning.


  • Digital Orientation & Apartment Management System

    • The digital orientation system involves all aspects for new students enrolment, and is a application for multi-application integration and full data sharing. The digital orientation system has improved the management level and management efficiency of the school and brought convenience to the students. Based on the student card and data exchange platform, the digital orientation system forms an integrated, intelligent, shared and “one-stop” service platform by integrating the card system, enrollment management system, financial management system and dormitory management system. New students can easily check the progress of the enrolment process.
